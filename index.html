Domineering Game

Game Rules

  • Players: This game is played between a player (you) and the computer.
  • Objective: The goal is to place your domino pieces on the board, blocking your opponent from making a move.
  • Player Move: You can place your domino pieces vertically.
  • Computer Move: The computer places its domino pieces horizontally.
  • Placing Dominoes: Each domino occupies two adjacent cells. Once a cell is occupied, it cannot be used again.
  • Winning Condition: The game ends when one player cannot make a valid move. The last player to successfully place a domino wins!

Now that you know the rules, go ahead and try to outsmart the computer!
